About Us

Engineering Excellence for Intelligent Manufacturing Founded in 2024, ANJANEYA AUTOMATION is dedicated to delivering cutting-edge automation solutions that improve efficiency, accuracy, and productivity across diverse industries. We design and manufacture advanced material handling systems, welding fixtures, coil lifters, transformer workstations, and special purpose machines engineered to meet modern industrial requirements. Our expertise ranges from automated systems that optimize production flow to custom-built machinery designed to solve complex operational challenges. Every project is executed with strong technical knowledge and a customer-focused mindset, ensuring solutions that are practical, reliable, and performance-driven. With a strong commitment to innovation and quality, ANJANEYA AUTOMATION serves the automotive industry,railway industry, transformer industry, food and pharma industry, petrochemical industry and manufacturing sectors with solutions tailored to their evolving needs. Powered by technology and guided by experience, we help businesses accelerate operations, enhance efficiency, and achieve superior results.
